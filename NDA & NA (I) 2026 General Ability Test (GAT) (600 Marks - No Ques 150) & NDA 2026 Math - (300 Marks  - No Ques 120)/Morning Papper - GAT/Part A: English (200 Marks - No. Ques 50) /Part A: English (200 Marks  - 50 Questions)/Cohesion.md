# NDA 2026 English Cohesion Notes

## Cohesion Skills
Cohesion refers to the logical connection between words, sentences, and ideas in a text. It includes ordering words in a sentence and ordering sentences in a paragraph (Para Jumbles).

### Ordering Words in a Sentence:
- Ensure subject-verb-object order.
- Adjectives before nouns, adverbs before verbs.
- Logical sequence: time, place, manner.

### Para Jumbles (Ordering Sentences in a Paragraph):
- Identify the opening sentence: Often introduces the topic.
- Look for connectors: However, Therefore, Moreover, etc.
- Pronouns: Refer back to previous sentences.
- Chronological or logical flow: Cause-effect, problem-solution.
- Closing sentence: Conclusion or summary.

### Tips:
- Read all sentences first.
- Find links: Words like "this," "that," "it" refer to prior mentions.
- Eliminate options by checking flow.
- Practice identifying themes and transitions.

### Types of Questions:
- Direct: What is the correct order?
- Inference: Which sentence cannot be first?
- Word order: Arrange words to form a correct sentence.

### Para Jumble 1
Sentences:  
1. The sun is a star at the center of our solar system.  
2. It provides light and heat to Earth.  
3. Planets orbit around it.  
4. Without it, life on Earth would not exist.  

1. What is the correct order?  
   A) 1-2-3-4  
   B) 1-3-2-4  
   C) 3-1-2-4  
   D) 4-1-2-3  
   **Answer:** A) 1-2-3-4  
   **Explanation:** Logical flow: Introduction, then details, conclusion.

2. Which sentence introduces the topic?  
   A) 1  
   B) 2  
   C) 3  
   D) 4  
   **Answer:** A) 1  
   **Explanation:** Direct.

3. What follows sentence 1?  
   A) 2  
   B) 3  
   C) 4

   **Answer:** A) 2  
   **Explanation:** Provides details.

4. Which cannot be last?  
   A) 4  
   B) 3  
   C) 2  
   D) 1  
   **Answer:** D) 1  
   **Explanation:** 1 is introductory.

5. Arrange words: star a is sun the.  
   A) The sun is a star.  
   B) A star is the sun.  
   C) Sun the is a star.  
   D) Is the sun a star.  
   **Answer:** A) The sun is a star.  
   **Explanation:** Subject-verb-object.

   C) To Earth provides It light and heat.

   C) Orbit planets around it.  
   D) It around orbit planets.  
   **Answer:** A) Planets orbit around it.
