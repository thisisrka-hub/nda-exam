# NDA 2026 English Comprehension Notes

## Comprehension Skills
Comprehension involves reading passages carefully and answering questions based on them. Questions can be direct (explicit) or inference-based (implied).

### Tips:
- Read the passage thoroughly first.
- Identify main ideas, tone, and key details.
- For direct questions: Find answers in the text.
- For inference: Use logic to deduce from context.
- Watch for words like "implies," "suggests," "can be inferred."
- Practice skimming for facts and scanning for details.

### Types:
- Factual: Who, what, when, where.
- Inference: Why, how, implications.
- Vocabulary: Meaning in context.
- Tone/Attitude: Author's view.
